MUSIC

THE MUSIC IS MADE BY MYSELF WITH THE KORG MS-20 MINI, A COUPLE OF EFFECT PEDALS (MOSTLY REVERB/DELAY) AND SAMPLES I RECORDED WITH THE ZOOM H1 RECORDER.
